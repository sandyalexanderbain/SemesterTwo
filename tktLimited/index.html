Ting Kok Tsuen

Small village in Hong Kong.

A village is a traditional Hong Kong residential living area. commonly found within the N.T. (New Territories).
The houses that a village is made up of are frequently larger and low-rise compared to those in the center of Hong Kong, HK island, concentrated with economical, political and historical aspects.

  1. The N.T. makes up 86% of Hong Kong's land with over 4 million residents.
  2. N.T. is nature dominated with 40% of the total area being country parks and nature reserves.
