Ting Kok Tsuen (TKT)

🇭🇰 TKT is a small local village within the Tai Po District of Hong Kong (HKSAR/HK). 🇭🇰

A local village is a traditional residential area, originally for local indigenous farmers. Still predominantly local indigenous farmers, though expansion and access for locals from highrise areas of HK as well as expats continues to increase. HK has 18 districts in total, all for the purpose of adminstrative and development planning, each district is represented by a distrinct council. Tai Po is 1 of 18.

Main Regions of HK

The NT holds 9 out of the 18 HK districts and is the most abundant main region to find local villages.
The houses that a village is made up of are frequently larger and low-rise compared to those in the center of Hong Kong, HK island, concentrated with economical, political and historical aspects.

  1. The NT makes up 86% of Hong Kong's land with over 4 million residents.
  2. NT is nature dominated, with 40% of the total area being country parks and nature reserves.
