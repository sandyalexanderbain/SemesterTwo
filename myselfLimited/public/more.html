More? Yes, it is that interesting.

One of the first days at boarding school in Italy reminded me of what I wanted to work for. Benjamin, my Chilean roommate, said to me to go to the "mensa" (school canteen) without him, and that I cannot always wait on him or someone to do things with. Not a supposed inherent purpose, rather I had also thought to myself over the summer that I will be in pursuit of confidence and conversational mastery.

In the beginning, it felt daunting yet I walked into the school canteen and sat at a table by myself alone. As people came in I felt a little nervous as to what others may think, then someone came and asked if they could eat breakfast with me. Esther, from Estwatini, a kind-hearted mellow individual. Thinking back straight after my breakfast meal with Esther and some other peers, I felt a strong conviction in the opportunities that existed. Initially, they seemed to not be there, yet with a little effort and active seeking of such opportunities, I realised the last thing I needed was to have faith in time. challenge myself to end up being confident in being conversation.

Now it is 2026, four years since starting boarding school. The confidence I possess currently is incomprehensible to the 16 year old me. Confidence that is not rooted in arrogance, yet true confidence in myself and a curious attitude open to the uncertain aspects of life.

I regret from boarding school is not having run for class representative, not out of the want for the title, out of the desire to give things a shot myself and be convicted in my ability to be distinct and challenge myself.

There is always something to be proud or dread upon. Here is a personal example of something I both feel proud on and think about what I could have done better.

TedxUWCAdriatic

By no means perfect, or necessarily a top tier ted talk. Remembering the moment before and after presenting, the execution I managed to deliever impressed me. I would love to take another opportunity like this in the future.

To create something personal, with my own thought, regardless of the originality, that is something I love. If I can notice a detail that is distinct, maybe some merit exists.
